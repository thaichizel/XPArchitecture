Bellair Station Fleche

After a terrible occurrence at Bellair Station, the community gathered to rebuild its historic fleche... This project became a remarkable example of heritage conservation and innovation in modern architecture.

Using Sketchup as a primary design tool, architects were able to recreate the intricate details of the structure while adapting it to modern standards. The restoration drew accolades from architects worldwide.
